Celestial Watchface Configuration

This is the configuration page for the Celestial Watchface. Feel free to play around with all of the options to match your unique style. Send me an email at zjijz11@gmail.com for new feature suggestions.
Color Selections
Not all color combinations will work when overlapping due to the limited colors available. Be sure to try many combinations to find your favorite!
Rotation Options
Be aware that smooth rotation will increase battery drain and decrease overall watch use time.
